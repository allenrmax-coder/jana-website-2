Accessibility Statement

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Mode on Main by Mara is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards to this website.

Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. This site aims to conform with WCAG 2.1 level AA.

"Partially conforms" means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard. We are actively working to bring all areas of this site into full conformance.

Measures we take

  • Use semantic HTML and clear page structure so assistive technologies can navigate the site.
  • Provide meaningful alternative text for images that convey information.
  • Maintain color-contrast ratios that meet WCAG AA requirements for body text.
  • Support full keyboard navigation for interactive elements (links, buttons, forms).
  • Honor user motion preferences — animations are reduced when the operating system requests prefers-reduced-motion.
  • Test against screen readers and keyboard-only navigation during design iterations.

Known limitations

Despite our best efforts, some content on this site may not yet be fully accessible. We are aware of the following limitations and are actively working to address them:

  • Some embedded third-party content (Shopify storefront, social media widgets) may not meet the same accessibility standards as our own content. We are working with our providers to improve this.
  • Older product imagery may not have detailed alt text descriptions.

Feedback & contact

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of this site. If you encounter an accessibility barrier, or you need information in an alternative format, please let us know — we aim to respond within 5 business days.
