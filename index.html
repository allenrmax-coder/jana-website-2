A Little Nostalgic.

Mode on Main by Mara is a curated lifestyle boutique in the heart of Old Town Fairfax, Virginia. Founded by Jana Klavina, the shop celebrates sustainable fashion and intentional shopping — blending vintage finds with contemporary pieces that feel authentic and alive.

Whether you're discovering your signature style or adding a timeless piece to your wardrobe, you'll always leave with something that feels like you.
